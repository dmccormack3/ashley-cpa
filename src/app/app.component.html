My Services

✓

Financial Planning Assistance

+
  • Individual Income Tax Filing: Preparing and filing federal, state, and local tax returns for individuals.
  • Tax Planning: Offering guidance on strategies to reduce tax liability throughout the year, such as deductions and credits.
  • Amended Returns: Assisting clients in correcting previously filed tax returns if errors were made.
  • E-filing: Submitting tax returns electronically to speed up the process and ensure accuracy.
  • Tax Resolution Services: Helping clients address issues with the IRS, including back taxes, penalties, and payment plans.
✓

Business Tax Preparation

+
  • Small Business Tax Returns: Preparing tax returns for sole proprietors, partnerships, LLCs, and S-corporations.
  • Business Tax Planning: Assisting business owners in planning for tax savings, deductions, and credits relevant to their business type.
  • Quarterly Estimated Taxes: Helping business owners calculate and file estimated tax payments to avoid penalties.
  • Sales Tax Filing: Preparing and filing sales tax returns for businesses that collect sales tax.
✓

Tax Advice and Consultation

+
  • Tax Consultation: Providing advice on specific tax-related questions or situations, such as how to handle investments, inheritances, or complex deductions.
  • Audit Representation: Representing clients during an audit with the IRS, including communication and documentation support.
✓

Specialized Tax Services

+
  • Tax Filing for Non-Residents: Assisting non-resident aliens with tax returns for U.S.-source income (Form 1040NR).
  • Self-Employed & Freelancers: Offering specialized tax preparation for self-employed individuals, including deductions for business expenses, home office, and vehicle use.
